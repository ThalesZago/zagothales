Every developer blog starts with a "Hello World" post. It's practically a tradition at this point.

## Why I Started Writing

I've been building software for a while now, and somewhere along the way I realized that writing is one of the most underrated tools in a developer's toolkit. Not just for documentation or commit messages — but for thinking through problems, sharing what you've learned, and connecting with others in the field.

This blog is my space to do all of that.

## What to Expect

No strict theme, but you can expect posts about:

- **Things I'm building** — notes from side projects, decisions made, lessons learned
- **Tools and workflows** — what's in my stack and why
- **Concepts I'm exploring** — deep dives into topics I'm curious about
- **Philosophy beyond code** - sometimes code is not enough

I'll try to keep posts focused and practical. No fluff, no filler.

## A Note on Consistency

I'm not committing to a posting schedule. Quality over cadence. When I have something worth saying, I'll say it.

If you find anything useful here, that's enough for me.
